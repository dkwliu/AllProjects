Seasteading

Check out this new and exciting living style of the future. Seasteading is a beneficial, and some would say necessary, system of living that is gaining a lot of traction from entrepreneurs and longterm thinkers alike. The basic idea of seasteading is to build a livable community on water. The benefits of such an arrangement varies and is dependent on who's asking, but the following is a list of general benefits:

-Great for people that need work visas, but have difficulty obtaining one. With seasteads, one can work for another country while staying on the seastead. -High economic potential. Seasteads can be used for tourism, working, and living. -Great aesthetics. Of course the first few seasteads will be made with aesthetics in mind.

And to keep with the theme of this site, there are also environmental benefits.

-Less impact on land. Because the communities are built off-land, this decreases potentially environmentally hazardous practices such as clear-cutting, habitat destruction, etc.. -Seasteads could promote more sustainable ways to generate energy. Since some seasteads would not be expected to be a part of an energy grid, this promotes the use of sustainable energy, such as wind and sunlight. -Seasteads are planned to be developed sustainably. Seastead organizations, such as Blueseed and Seasteading Institute, are thinking of developing seasteads with sustainable materials and that are capable of sustaining itself.
